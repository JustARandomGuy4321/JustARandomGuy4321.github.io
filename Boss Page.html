Bosses

There are many bosses in Mega Boss Survival. They have difficulties, which are
Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard, Impossible, Beyond Inmaginable, Godly, Mythic,
Omega, and Infinity.
If you want to survive the bosses, you will need to use strategies. For bosses
ranging from Easy to Godly, a strategy could be to find shelter, or go on a ceiling
of a structure. For bosses ranging from Mythic to Infinity, your best strategy is
to just run. For example, the Witherstorm has an aura that removes your health,
and floats around. Run. Just Run.
There is only one Infinity boss, and it is the God Kraken. It is almost impossible
to survive on land, because its 300 tentacles grab you and fling you away,
making you take terrifying damage. Your best strategy is to hitchhike a UFO or
a spaceship, making you go in the air, where the God Kraken cannot get you.
