Mechs

There are things you can drive, and there are currently 3 types. Mechs, Jaegars,
and Dragons. There are many mechs, and all of them have a sword. Some
obtainable mechs are the angel mech, which you obtain by climbing the Stairs of Heaven.
Obtain the stone mech by finding it in the treasure island. It is the
same case with the grass mech, water mech and sand mech.
Jaegars are basically huge mechs, and there are currently four Jaegars in the
game. They are the Gipsy Danger, Obsidian Fury, Walking House, and Purple
Walking House.
Dragons are extremely rare, and they fly. There are also four dragons in the
game. The Default Dragon, the Ice Dragon, The Black Dragon, and The
Radioactive Dragon.
There is also the UFO, and the Spaceships, which you can buy for 9 million points.
The Mothership is the rarest mode of transportation. Only 23 people
have it, and that number is very likely to stay that way for a long time.
